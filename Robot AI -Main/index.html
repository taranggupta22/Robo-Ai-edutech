Industrial Project

Create Your SPOT (Agile mobile Robot)

Inspired by Boston Dynamics, mentees undertake a credit industrial major project: creating their own Agile Mobile Robot, SPOT. Guided by mentors, we merge cutting-edge engineering, AI, and real-world application.

Through rigorous design, testing, and iterations, we aim to develop a groundbreaking SPOT-like robot that autonomously navigates complex environments, performs tasks, and adapts to dynamic scenarios. This project showcases their mastery of robotics, problem-solving skills, and dedication to pushing boundaries in robotics engineering.
